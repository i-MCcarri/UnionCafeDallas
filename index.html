A Little Bit About Union...

The idea for Union started with dreaming-out-loud conversations between Mike Baughman, Neil Moseley and Phil Dieke.

Launched as a laboratory and new church start to experiment with three key things:

  • 1) Learning by embedding within the neighborhood.
  • 2) Exploring alternative means of sustaining good work.
  • 3) Engaging young professionals who have left the church.

Since opening in 2012, we’ve developed a top-rated independent coffee shop and three worshipping communities.
