Our Mission

Union cultivates the divine spark in our neighbors for the good of the city and the world it inspires through outstanding coffee, robust community and engaging causes.

Our Core Values

Apostleship

We don’t adhere to ‘the way things have been done,’ but entrepreneurially take old pathways and bring them to new communities, contexts, schools of thought, conversations and paradigms. We construct new matter from old materials.

Boundary-Breaking

We embrace playful, constructive ways to color outside the lines that separate people from people, institutions from institutions and the present from the future. We poke social divides and peer through holes to see what is on the other side.

Generosity

We offer our vulnerability, our resources, our time, our voice, our privilege, our light, our hope, our story and ourselves. So that Dallas might be better. So that the world might be better.

Quality

When there are few standards to follow, we will be the standard against which others are measured. We set an example by our bold failures and brilliant successes.

Sanctuary

Sanctuary is more than a place. We must choose sanctuary in order to offer refuge to those who feel threatened. Sanctuary dwells in people and swims in cups of coffee that tell us we are okay.

Storytelling

We tell stories on stages, around tables, behind counters, online, and most importantly, with the decisions we make. We tell our own stories and the stories that make us who we are.

Sustainability

We attend to and craft ecosystems that nourish the long-term health of that which is alive—communities, people, plants, ideas and dreams.
